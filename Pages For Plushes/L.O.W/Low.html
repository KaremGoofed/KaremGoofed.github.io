Cheesy and the Gang

The Little Organized Warbot, in short, Low. An 7 year old doll-like child. Coming from species called The Atoms, Low was born way back in 1899, by a brilliant scientist who deconstructed an Atoms corpse and fused it with metal found on Earth. After being shut off in 1903, Low would stay shut down for many years up until recently. He loves to play with others, his favorite holiday is Halloween and is afraid of reindeers.
