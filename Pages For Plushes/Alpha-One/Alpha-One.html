Alpha-One

Cheesy and the Gang

Low's older sister, Alpha-One is the first ever inanimate object to possess human emotions, as well as being the only living thing to turn herself into both a solid and liquid on command. When she's brought into a fight, her tentacles emerge from her back, making Alpha erupt in rage. The only thing Alpha really cares about is Zebra Cakes and her love interest, Elite.
