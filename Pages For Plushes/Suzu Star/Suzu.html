Suzu Star

Cheesy and the Gang

The older sister of Bliss Star. Suzu owns a hotel which has a worth value of over millions. She lives comfortably in luxery, usually spends her time alone. She is overly protective of her younger sister, which deems her aggressive and hostile when it comes to Bliss. She is highly unsupportive of Bliss' relationship with Gisby Jude.
