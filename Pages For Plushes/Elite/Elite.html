Elite

Cheesy and the Gang

Elite comes from the feared species, The Atoms. Within his people, Elite is widely known for his deformity, his whole head acts as his mouth. He likes to roll on the ground, find flowers to pat, and loves to oink whenever he sees a star in the sky. When threatened, Elite will open his mouth, revealing his sharp metallic teeth, and his long extendable tongue, and use it to attack his target. His love intrest is Alpha-one.
